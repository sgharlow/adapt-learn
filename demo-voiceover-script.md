---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Key Points to Emphasize:
- **ElevenLabs** - Powers all audio narration and AI Tutor responses
- **Google Gemini** - Powers the AI Tutor's intelligent responses
- **Adaptive Learning** - Gap detection and personalized recommendations
- **Voice-First** - Learn without looking at a screen

### Tone:
- Conversational and enthusiastic
- Clear and confident
- Professional but approachable

### Pacing:
- Allow natural pauses between sections
- Match narration to on-screen actions
- Slow down for key feature demonstrations

### Suggested Background Music:
- Upbeat, modern, tech-forward
- Low volume under narration
- Fade up during transitions
