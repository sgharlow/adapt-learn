## Pre-Recording Checklist

### Technical Setup
- [ ] Close unnecessary browser tabs and applications
- [ ] Use Chrome/Edge in incognito mode (clean state)
- [ ] Set browser zoom to 100%
- [ ] Resolution: 1920x1080 recommended
- [ ] Clear browser cache/localStorage for fresh experience
- [ ] Test audio output (speakers/headphones working)
- [ ] Microphone working for voiceover
- [ ] Screen recording software ready (OBS, Loom, or similar)

### Browser Prep
- [ ] Open https://adapt-learn-rosy.vercel.app in a fresh tab
- [ ] Have a second tab ready at /lesson/ml-fundamentals-01 (optional)
- [ ] Disable browser notifications
- [ ] Hide bookmarks bar for cleaner look

### Audio Check
- [ ] Test lesson audio plays (click play on any lesson)
- [ ] Test AI Tutor responds (ask a question)
- [ ] Adjust system volume to appropriate level

- AI Explorer (Beginner)

### FEATURE 2: Audio Lessons (60 seconds)

**[Screen: Lesson page - ml-fundamentals-01]**

> "Here's a lesson on 'What is Machine Learning?' Notice the audio player at the top - this is a 4-minute lesson generated using ElevenLabs' text-to-speech."

**Action:** Click PLAY and let it play for 5-10 seconds

> "The audio quality is natural and engaging - perfect for learning on the go. You can adjust playback speed, skip forward or back, and control volume."

**Action:** Demonstrate:
1. Click speed button to show 1x, 1.5x, 2x options

3. Pause the audio

> "Below the player, you have the full lesson content with learning objectives, structured sections, and key takeaways - so you can follow along or review later."

**Action:** Scroll through the lesson content briefly

### FEATURE 3: AI Tutor - Voice Q&A (90 seconds) **[KEY FEATURE]**

**[Screen: Lesson page with AI Tutor panel]**

> "And here's the magic - the response is automatically converted to speech using ElevenLabs, so you can listen to the answer just like the lesson."

> "This creates a truly conversational learning experience. You can ask follow-up questions, request simpler explanations, or ask for more examples."

**Action:** Click "Tell me more" or type a follow-up question

## Key Talking Points to Emphasize

### ElevenLabs Integration (Required for hackathon)
1. **Pre-generated lesson audio** - 42 lessons, ~440MB of high-quality voice content
2. **Real-time TTS for AI responses** - Tutor answers converted to speech instantly
3. **Natural, engaging voice** - Makes learning feel like a podcast

### Google Gemini Integration (Required for hackathon)
1. **Context-aware tutoring** - Understands the current lesson content
2. **Conversational Q&A** - Natural follow-up questions
3. **Concise responses** - Optimized for audio delivery (under 200 words)

### Unique Value Proposition
- "Learn AI like you listen to podcasts"
- Voice-first design for on-the-go learning
- Personalized AI tutor available 24/7
- Adaptive learning based on quiz performance

## Recording Tips

1. **Speak slowly and clearly** - Easier to follow and edit
2. **Pause between sections** - Helps with editing
3. **Show, don't just tell** - Click things, demonstrate features
4. **Keep mouse movements smooth** - Avoid jerky scrolling
5. **Enthusiasm matters** - Sound excited about what you built!
